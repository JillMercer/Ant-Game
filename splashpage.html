Directions for Ant Race Game


1. You can chose which level difficulty you want. Either easy, medium, or impossible.
2.Your goal is to drag as many ants as you can to the jar displayed on the screen.
3. You get more points if you grab faster moving ants.
4. If you manage to collect them all you are redirected to a YOU WON page
5. The time limit for each level is different. For the easy level you have 30 seconds. For the medium level you have 20 seconds, and for the impossible level you have 15 seconds.
6. When time is up, you will be directed to a new page. On the new page, your final score will be displayed.

Google Chrome is the browser that is being used for this project. We got the jar image from http://www.clker.com/clipart-plain-dream-jar-2.html and out tile background from www.franchise-shop.It/page/2/.
The gif ants were made by Dustin Fisch.

Animation Techniques

The animation techniques that were used for this project were drag/ drop, and moving animation across the screen.
